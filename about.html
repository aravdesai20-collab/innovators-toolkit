About

A student-built resource for students

My name is Arav Desai, and I am a student at Independence High School, where I am enrolled in the Independent Study and Mentorship (ISM) course. This website is my original work and represents the research, interviews, and exploration I have done around entrepreneurship, startups, and the role of AI in innovation. Through ISM, I've had the opportunity to learn directly from mentors and professionals, and this site is my way of turning that learning into a resource other students can use and build from.
