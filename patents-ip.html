Patents & IP Basics

Understanding intellectual property for startups

Intellectual property is basically legal rights over things you create. Patents protect inventions, copyrights automatically protect your code, and trademarks protect your brand name. Most student projects don't need patents, but understanding the basics helps you make better decisions.
